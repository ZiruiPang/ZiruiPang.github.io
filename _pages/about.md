Pang Zirui is a current student at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, pursuing a Master’s degree in Predictive Analytics and Risk Management in the College of Liberal Arts & Sciences, Department of Mathematics. He is also a member of the iRisk Lab at the same university. He completed his undergraduate studies at Central University of Finance and Economics, where he majored in Data Science and Big Data Technology in the School of Statistics and Mathematics. Through his past research projects and internship experiences, Pang Zirui has developed a strong interest in the application of machine learning algorithms, data science, and actuarial science in finance, as well as quantitative finance. He is actively seeking research projects and research assistant positions in these areas and is highly motivated to pursue a Ph.D. or research role in the future.
